Project Title: Bookstore Inventory Management System

Description:
Create a bookstore inventory management system using Python and SQL. 
The system will allow users to perform various operations such as adding new books to the inventory, 
updating book information, deleting books, and searching for books based on different criteria.

Features:

Book Entry: Users can add new books to the inventory by providing details such as title, author, genre, ISBN, and quantity.
Book Update: Users can update the information of existing books, such as changing the quantity or updating the author's name.
Book Deletion: Users can remove books from the inventory.
Book Search: Users can search for books based on different criteria, such as title, author, or genre.
View Inventory: Users can view the entire inventory, including all book details and quantities.
Sales Management: Users can track sales by recording the number of books sold and updating the inventory accordingly.
